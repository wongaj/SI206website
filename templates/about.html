About Cobab's Pizza

Cobab's Pizza is a purely fictional pizza restaurant.

Cobab's Pizza doesn't exist in real life and is the brainchild of Alden Wong.
The inspiration for Cobab's Pizza comes from the phrase "Cobab's trend is toward less formal clothing".
The focus for Cobab's Pizza is towards less formal cuisine and it starts with pizza.
Enjoy Cobab's.
